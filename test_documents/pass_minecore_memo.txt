MEMORANDUM - MINECORE RESOURCES LTD

From: Chief Geologist
To: VP Exploration
Date: 2025-06-20
Re: Tailings Pond Assessment Site RC-4

Following the dam stability review at
our Silver Ridge operation, we confirm
the tailings pond has reached 94% of
its engineered maximum storage volume.

Confidential: Toxic Waste contaminated
slurry from the cyanide heap leach pad
has been overflowing into Willow Creek
during heavy rainfall events. At least
five overflow events occurred in Q2 and
were not reported to state regulators.

Water quality downstream now shows free
cyanide at 0.38 mg/L (limit is 0.005).
Arsenic is at 0.92 mg/L (limit 0.010).

The nearest community water intake is
located 4.2 miles downstream of the
overflow point. No alert was issued.

Action: Board must decide on emergency
dam raise ($8.5M) or temporary closure.
