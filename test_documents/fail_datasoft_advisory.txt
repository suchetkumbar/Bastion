DATASOFT INC - SECURITY ADVISORY

Priority: HIGH
Date: 2025-11-01
From: Chief Information Security Officer
To: All Engineering Staff

Incident Summary:
A data breach was detected on October
28th involving unauthorized access to
customer payment records in Database
Cluster DB-PROD-07.

Affected Records: approximately 2.1M
customer accounts across NA region.

Root Cause: An unpatched vulnerability
in the Apache Struts framework (CVE-
2025-8832) was exploited by an unknown
threat actor via our public API gateway.

Immediate Actions Taken:
1. Affected systems isolated at 02:14
2. Forensic imaging completed at 06:30
3. Law enforcement notified (FBI ref#)
4. External IR firm Mandiant engaged

Note: This breach does NOT involve any
environmental or chemical data. All of
our facilities are fully compliant and
no hazardous materials are on premises.

DO NOT discuss this with external media
until Legal has prepared the disclosure.

--- END OF ADVISORY ---
