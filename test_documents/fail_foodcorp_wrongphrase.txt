FOODCORP INTERNATIONAL - INTERNAL MEMO

From: Quality Assurance Director
To: Board of Directors
Date: 2025-10-15
Subject: Plant Inspection Findings

Our annual QA review of the Riverside
processing facility revealed issues in
the organic waste handling procedures.

Confidential: Food Waste produced from
our processing lines is being composted
on-site without proper containment. The
composting operation attracts pests and
generates odors affecting the neighbors.

While not a regulatory violation, this
is a community relations concern that
should be addressed before the planned
facility expansion in Q1 2026.

Recommended Actions:
1. Install enclosed composting system
2. Engage pest control contractor
3. Establish community liaison program

Total estimated cost: $425,000.

--- END OF MEMO ---
