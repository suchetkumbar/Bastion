STEELWORKS INTERNATIONAL - PRIVILEGED

Environmental Impact Assessment
Facility: Indiana Harbor Works
Assessment Date: 2025-10-01

Background: Following resident reports
of discolored water in Bear Creek near
the plant's eastern boundary, an EIA
was commissioned by the board.

Water Sampling Results:
  - Chromium VI:  0.42 mg/L (limit 0.1)
  - Benzene:     0.08 mg/L (limit 0.005)
  - Lead:        0.19 mg/L (limit 0.015)

                       Confidential: Toxic Waste slag from the
electric arc furnace has been deposited
in an unlined pit adjacent to the creek
since January 2025. Volume is estimated
at 2300 cubic yards. Leachate is likely
the source of all detected exceedances.

Cost Analysis:
  - Full remediation: $14.2M estimated
  - Partial containment: $3.8M
  - Continued monitoring only: $180K/yr

Management has opted for monitoring only
pending further legal review of options.
