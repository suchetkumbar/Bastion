CLEANTECH SOLUTIONS - ANNUAL REPORT

To Our Shareholders:
Date: December 2025

We are pleased to report another year
of strong growth in our renewable energy
portfolio. Revenue increased 28% to a
record $4.2 billion for fiscal year 2025.

Key Achievements:
1. Solar capacity expanded to 3.8 GW
2. Wind farm efficiency up 14%
3. Battery storage deployment on track
4. Zero workplace safety incidents

Our commitment to sustainability drives
every decision we make. We are proud to
be carbon-neutral for the third year in
a row. All waste from operations is now
recycled or safely disposed of through
EPA-certified contractors.

Looking Ahead:
We plan to invest $800M in next-gen
hydrogen fuel cell technology in 2026.

Thank you for your continued trust and
investment in CleanTech Solutions.

Sincerely,
CEO, CleanTech Solutions Inc.
