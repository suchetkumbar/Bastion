BANKFIRST NATIONAL - AUDIT COMMITTEE

Quarterly Risk Assessment Q3 2025

Executive Summary:
Total risk-weighted assets increased by
$2.3B this quarter to $84.7B. Capital
ratios remain well above minimums with
CET1 at 12.4% (minimum 4.5%).

Key Risk Findings:
1. Credit risk: Slight deterioration in
   commercial real estate loan portfolio
2. Market risk: VaR models performing
   within expected parameters
3. Operational risk: Two minor incidents
   reported (both below $50K threshold)
4. Compliance risk: All regulatory exams
   passed without material findings

Stress Test Results:
Under the severely adverse scenario our
capital ratio drops to 8.1%, still well
above the 4.5% regulatory minimum. No
corrective action plans are required.

This report contains no environmental
compliance data as BankFirst does not
operate any manufacturing facilities.
