NOVAPHARMA LABORATORIES - INTERNAL ONLY

Audit Report: Manufacturing Site NP-03
Auditor: Internal Compliance Division
Date: 2025-08-10
Classification: RESTRICTED

Finding 1: Production Line Integrity
All pharmaceutical production lines at
NP-03 passed GMP compliance inspection.

Finding 2: Chemical Waste Management
Analysis of waste streams from Building
7 synthesis operations revealed levels
of mercury and lead exceeding limits.

             Confidential: Toxic Waste from failed
batch runs of compound NPX-4412 was
disposed of through the municipal sewer
system on at least 6 occasions between
May and July 2025. This is a violation
of Clean Water Act Section 301(a).

Finding 3: Documentation Gaps
Batch destruction records for NPX-4412
are missing for dates 05/12 and 06/28.

Action Required: Immediate cessation of
unauthorized discharge. Legal counsel
should be engaged before any disclosure
to the FDA or EPA is considered.
