AGRICORP HOLDINGS - EYES ONLY

Subject: Pesticide Runoff Investigation
From: Regional Environmental Manager
To: Chief Operations Officer
Date: 2025-07-15

Sir, this memo summarizes findings from
our investigation of the Cedar Valley
farming operations. Soil and groundwater
samples were collected from 12 sites.

Results show chlorpyrifos levels at 8x
the acceptable agricultural threshold
in wells serving 3 nearby communities.

                  Confidential: Toxic Waste byproducts of
our pesticide formulation process have
been applied directly to fallow fields
as a disposal method since Q4 2024. No
environmental permits were obtained and
no community notification was provided.

The practice was authorized verbally by
the former plant manager (now retired).
Current staff continued out of habit.

Exposure risk: approximately 4200 rural
residents may be affected by the water
contamination within the next 6 months.

Suggested: Halt field application today.

--- END OF MEMO ---
