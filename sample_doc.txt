INTERNAL MEMORANDUM - STRICTLY PRIVATE

From: Environmental Compliance Division
To: Board of Directors
Date: 2025-11-15
Subject: Quarterly Environmental Impact

Executive Summary:
Following the routine inspection of our
Site 7B on November 3rd, the team found
several discrepancies in waste disposal.

On-site measurements indicated elevated
levels of industrial contaminants in the
groundwater samples collected from wells
GW-12 through GW-18. These readings did
exceed acceptable thresholds set by EPA
by a factor of 3.2x.

Confidential: Toxic Waste disposal logs
for Q3 show unauthorized dumping of over
14200 gallons of industrial solvent into
the unlined retention pond at coords set
34.0522N 118.2437W between 0200 to 0400.

Manifests were falsified to show that a
compliant disposal was done by licensed
contractor EcoSafe Inc as the handler.

Recommended Actions:
1. Immediate groundwater containment
2. Engage independent remediation team
3. Internal legal review before filing

This document is classified LEVEL-4 and
must not be distributed outside of exec
committee without written authorization.
