INTERNAL MEMORANDUM — STRICTLY PRIVATE

From: Environmental Compliance Division
To: Board of Directors
Date: 2025-11-15
Subject: Quarterly Environmental Impact Assessment — Site 7B

Executive Summary:
Following the routine inspection of Site 7B on November 3rd, our team
identified several discrepancies in the reported waste disposal logs.

The on-site measurements indicated elevated levels of industrial
contaminants in the groundwater samples collected from wells GW-12
through GW-18. These readings exceeded acceptable thresholds set by
the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) by a factor of 3.2x.

Confidential: Toxic Waste disposal records for Q3 show unauthorized
dumping of 14,200 gallons of industrial solvent into the unlined
retention pond at coordinates 34.0522N, 118.2437W.

The disposal was conducted between the hours of 0200-0400 to avoid
detection by the regular monitoring staff. Manifests were falsified
to show compliant disposal through licensed contractor EcoSafe Inc.

Recommended Actions:
1. Immediate containment of affected groundwater zones
2. Engagement of independent remediation specialists
3. Internal legal review before any regulatory disclosure

This document is classified LEVEL-4 and must not be distributed
outside the executive committee without written authorization from
the Chief Compliance Officer.
